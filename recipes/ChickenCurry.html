Chicken Curry

Description

A flavorful and aromatic chicken curry, featuring tender pieces of chicken simmered in a rich, spiced sauce.

Some variations include coconut milk for a creamy texture, or yogurt for a tangy note. Garnished with fresh cilantro, it's a delicious and comforting dish, perfect with rice or naan bread.

Cooking Time:50 mins

Serves:3

Ingredients

  • ⅛ cup olive oil
  • 1 large onions, diced
  • ⅙ cup curry powder, or to taste
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into strips

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add onion and sauté until soft and golden brown, 5 to 8 minutes.
  2. Slowly stir in curry powder until well blended.
  3. Add chicken; reduce the heat to low, cover, and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, 20 to 40 minutes
  4. Uncover and cook, stirring constantly to prevent burning, until pan juices reduce to desired amount, 3 to 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with garlic Naan or plain Basmati rice
